About KUSU

Who we are

KUSU is the registered union for non-teaching university staff across public universities in Kenya. We stand for dignity, fairness and workers’ rights.

Mission

Organize, protect and advocate for better working conditions, fair treatment and staff welfare.

Values

Unity • Solidarity • Integrity • Equity • Accountability

Who we represent

ICT, Admin, Library, Technical, Security, Drivers, Support Staff — all public universities & constituent colleges.

Why Join

Your Rights. Your Voice. Your Power.

Union membership provides collective bargaining, legal support, protection and bargaining power — much stronger than going alone.

Legal Protection

Representation in disputes, hearings, and collective bargaining for fair terms.

Benefits & Conditions

Advocate for fair pay, allowances, leave, medical cover and working conditions.

Solidarity & Voice

A united voice carries stronger influence with management and authorities.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for common questions from staff and members.

Can I be punished for joining KUSU?
No. The Constitution of Kenya protects freedom of association and labour rights. No worker should be victimised, threatened or dismissed for joining a union or participating in lawful union activities.
How do I become a member?
Fill in the official KUSU membership form, provide your correct staff details and submit it to your branch officials for processing and payroll deduction. You can also contact KUSU using the form below for guidance.
What should I do if I face intimidation?
Document what has happened, keep any written evidence, and immediately inform your branch officials or the KUSU national office. Do not resign under pressure without seeking advice.
